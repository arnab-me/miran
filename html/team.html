Our Mission & Values

We are a diverse team of machine learning specialists and developers united by a common goal: to make emotional support accessible to everyone, anytime. Our interdisciplinary approach ensures that Miran combines cutting-edge AI technology with compassionate mental health support.

Every team member brings unique expertise to create an experience that is both technologically advanced and deeply human-centered. We believe that mental health support should be available whenever and wherever it's needed, without stigma or barriers.

Compassion First

Every interaction is designed with empathy and understanding at its core. We prioritize emotional intelligence in our AI development.

Technical Excellence

We maintain the highest standards in our AI development and infrastructure, ensuring reliable, secure support for our users.

Accessibility

Breaking down barriers to mental health support for all, regardless of location, background, or financial means.

Remember: While Miran can provide valuable support, it's not a replacement for professional mental health care in crisis situations. If you're in crisis, please contact a licensed professional or emergency services.
