Frequently Asked Questions

What is Miran and how does it work?

Miran is an AI-powered mental wellness companion that provides 24/7 support through conversational interactions. It uses natural language processing to understand your concerns and offers evidence-based responses to help with stress, anxiety, and emotional well-being.

Is Miran a replacement for therapy?

No, Miran is not a replacement for professional therapy or medical treatment. It's designed to provide supplemental support and should not be used as a substitute for professional mental health care when needed.

How is my privacy protected?

We prioritize your privacy. Conversations are encrypted and we don't store personally identifiable information. You can chat anonymously, and all data is handled according to strict privacy protocols.

What platforms is Miran available on?

Currently, Miran is available on Telegram as a chatbot. We're working on expanding to other platforms in the future to make our services more accessible.

Is there a cost to use Miran?

Miran currently offers free basic support. We may introduce premium features in the future, but core mental wellness support will remain accessible to all users.

Remember: While Miran can provide valuable support, it's not a replacement for professional mental health care in crisis situations. If you're in crisis, please contact a licensed professional or emergency services.
